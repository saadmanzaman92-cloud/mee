Research Poster-1

Cooling and Heat Management Systems in Electric Vehicles (EVs)

Presented By:
Sadman Zaman
Event:
International Conference on Sustainable Energy Systems
Date:
Oct 2025

Objective

Electric Vehicles (EVs) are transforming modern transportation by replacing internal combustion engines with electric powertrains. However, EV components—especially battery packs, power electronics, and motors—generate significant heat during operation. To maintain performance, efficiency, and safety, effective cooling and heat management systems are essential. These systems are designed using mechanical principles of heat transfer, fluid dynamics, and material selection to regulate temperature within optimal limits.
