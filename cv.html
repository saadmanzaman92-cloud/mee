Career Summary

I'm Sadman Zaman, currently pursuing my undergraduate degree in Mechanical Engineering at the Military Institute of Science and Technology (MIST). From the beginning of my academic journey, I have been driven by a passion for understanding how things work, solving complex problems, and transforming innovative ideas into tangible solutions. My curiosity and determination have guided me through a variety of challenging projects, internships, and personal initiatives, all aimed at strengthening my technical knowledge, practical skills, and professional growth..

My experience also extends to web-based and software-controlled systems, including automation projects where I integrated RC vehicles with camera systems, stepper motors, and live-streaming capabilities. These projects required a combination of mechanical design, electronics, programming, and problem-solving, allowing me to develop a multidisciplinary skill set and learn the importance of precision, reliability, and user-centric design.

I am particularly passionate about designing systems that integrate mechanical engineering principles with software and automation to create practical, innovative solutions. My career goal is to contribute to industries where engineering innovation meets real-world challenges—whether it's in robotics, aerospace, automation, or smart manufacturing. I aim to work on projects that push technological boundaries, improve efficiency, and make a meaningful impact on society.

In every project I undertake, I strive to combine technical excellence with creativity, curiosity, and resilience. I am motivated by the opportunity to solve challenging problems, learn continuously, and contribute to engineering solutions that are not only functional but transformative. With each experience, I grow as a professional and as an innovator, prepared to take on complex engineering challenges and deliver impactful results.
